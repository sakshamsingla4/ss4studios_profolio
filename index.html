✦ About

The Studio

SS4 Studios is an independent creative studio founded by Saksham Singla — dedicated to building mythological fantasy universes through the art of writing and animation.

Every story born here carries the weight of the divine. Our debut novel, The Gods Requested the Boy, is a reincarnation fantasy epic available in both English and Hindi — reaching readers across platforms worldwide.

We are currently in production on an anime adaptation, with original episodes coming soon to our YouTube channel. The universe is just beginning.
